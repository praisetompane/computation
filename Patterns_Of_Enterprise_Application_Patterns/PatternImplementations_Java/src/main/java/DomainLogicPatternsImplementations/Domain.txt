Revenue Recognition

    When can you actually COUNT the money you receive on your books?

    Company X
        Sells : Word processors, Databases, and Spreadsheets


        Revenue recognition rules on signing a contract
            1. Word processors: book all revenue immediately

            2. Spreadsheets:
                1 Third today
                1 Third in Sixty days (60)
                1 Third in Ninety days (90)

            3. Databases:
                1 Third today
                1 Third in Thirty days (30)
                1 Third in Sixty days (60)
